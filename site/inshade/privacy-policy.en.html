Privacy Policy (inShade)

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Forte, Inc. ("we") sets out how personal information and data are handled in the iOS / Android app "inShade" (the "App").

inShade is a walking navigation app that suggests routes with more shade rather than the shortest path. Route finding and shade calculation are performed on your device.

Information We Collect and Use

  • Location: Used to calculate shaded walking routes from your current location to your destination. Shade calculation is performed on-device. Your location and search terms may be sent to Apple and Google to display the map and search for places. Your location is never stored on our servers.
  • Search terms: Sent to Apple (MapKit) and Google to search for places.
  • Advertising identifier: On the free plan, Google AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier to serve ads. In the EEA, UK and similar regions, this follows consent management (UMP). Users who purchase the ad-free plan are not shown ads.
  • Purchase information: In-app purchases are processed by Apple (StoreKit) or Google (Play Billing). We do not obtain or store payment information (such as card numbers).
  • Access logs: To deliver shade data (tiles), standard access information such as IP addresses is temporarily recorded by our delivery infrastructure (Cloudflare). This is used only to prevent abuse and monitor performance.

On-Device Processing

Route finding and shade calculation are performed on your device. No account is required to use the App, and we do not create a profile that identifies you individually.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Data handling by each service follows its respective privacy policy.

  • Apple (map display and place search / in-app purchases)
  • Google (map display and place search / advertising via Google AdMob)
  • Cloudflare (delivery of shade data)

Analytics and Crash Reporting

As of the last updated date of this policy, we do not use any first-party analytics or crash reporting. If we introduce such tools in the future, we will update this policy and do so in a privacy-conscious manner.

Regional Handling (GDPR / CCPA, etc.)

For users in the EEA and the UK, we obtain consent for advertising through a consent management platform (UMP). Because the App has no accounts, we do not build cross-site tracking profiles. To exercise data rights under the laws of your region, please contact us using the address below.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
